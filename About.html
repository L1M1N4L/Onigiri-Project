Welcome to Onigiri: Your Pathway to Japanese Language Mastery

Inception and Passionate Founders

Founded on September 12, 2023, by three passionate individuals—Adrian, Jonathan, and Kevin Jonathan—Onigiri stands as the epitome of a commitment to quality education in the realm of Japanese language acquisition. As an integral part of the esteemed Kouyou Language Institute group, our platform is dedicated to delivering a diverse array of recorded courses and interactive quizzes designed to foster an immersive learning environment for Japanese language enthusiasts.

Philosophy of Hands-On Proficiency

At Onigiri, our philosophy is deeply rooted in the notion that true proficiency in the Japanese language arises from hands-on experience. Guided by this vision, we endeavor to offer an online learning experience that encapsulates the collective expertise of our founders—Adrian, Jonathan, and Kevin Jonathan—each bringing a wealth of experience and a shared dedication to providing practical, invaluable learning opportunities in Japanese linguistics.

Transformative Learning Experience

Our aim at Onigiri is to create an inclusive space where learners passionate about the Japanese language can explore various aspects of this rich linguistic tapestry. Through meticulously curated courses and engaging interactive quizzes, we aspire to transform the learning journey into an enjoyable and immersive experience—one that's abundant with cultural insights, linguistic knowledge, and practical application.

Embark on a Journey of Enrichment

Join us at Onigiri and embark on an educational journey designed to empower and enrich your understanding of the Japanese language. Let's dive into a world of linguistic nuances and cultural richness, all within the comfort of our engaging online platform.

Welcome to Onigiri—where the art of learning Japanese language meets firsthand experience.
