About the Course

Onigiri is your gateway to mastering the Japanese language. Our online course provides a comprehensive learning experience for beginners, focusing on the essentials of listening, speaking, reading, and writing in Japanese.

Discover the beauty of the Japanese language through engaging lessons designed to build a solid foundation. From basic greetings to everyday conversations, Onigiri covers essential grammar, vocabulary, and cultural insights to make your learning experience rich and meaningful.
